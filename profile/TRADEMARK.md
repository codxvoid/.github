# CodxVoid™ Brand & Trademark Policy
**Version 1.0 — February 2026**

---

## 1. Introduction
The mission of **CodxVoid** is to foster "Infinite Innovation" through open-source collaboration. To protect this mission, we must ensure that our identity—including our name, logo, and slogans—is used only in ways that promote the project and do not mislead the community.

While our source code is distributed under open-source licenses (such as GPLv3), those licenses **do not** include a sub-license to use our brand identity or trademarks.

## 2. Our Marks
This policy covers the following "Marks," held under common law and established via **MSME/Udyam Registration**:
* The name: **CodxVoid™**
* The slogan: **"Code Infinite, Think Void"™**
* The logo: The **Xfinity-style / Infinity** brand mark and associated iconography.
* The organization name: **CodzLab™**

## 3. Permitted Use
You are encouraged to use the name **CodxVoid** in the following ways:
* **Truthful Statements:** To describe that your software is "built for," "compatible with," or "runs on" CodxVoid technologies.
* **Attribution:** To credit the originating authors as required by the software license.
* **Community Discussion:** In blog posts, tutorials, or social media discussing the project and its development.

## 4. Prohibited Use
The following uses are strictly prohibited without prior written consent from the CodxVoid administration:
* **Confusing Similarity:** Using a name or logo that is "confusingly similar" to CodxVoid for a different software project.
* **False Affiliation:** Using the Marks in a way that implies an official partnership, endorsement, or sponsorship by CodxVoid.
* **Commercial Branding:** Using the logo or tagline on commercial products, advertisements, or marketing materials.
* **Unauthorized Registration:** Attempting to trademark, register, or claim ownership of these Marks (or derivatives) in any jurisdiction.

## 5. Naming Convention for Derivatives
If you fork a CodxVoid repository or create a derivative work:
* **Allowed:** Name your project something unique (e.g., *"VoidRunner — based on CodxVoid"*).
* **Not Allowed:** Using the brand in the primary title (e.g., *"CodxVoid Enterprise"* or *"Official CodxVoid Pro"*).

## 6. Legal Foundation
This policy is maintained by the **CodxVoid MSME entity**. We reserve the right to revoke permission to use our Marks if the usage is deemed detrimental to the project’s reputation or violates the terms above.

For permissions or inquiries regarding brand usage, please reach out via our official [GitHub Organization Profile](https://github.com/codxvoid).
